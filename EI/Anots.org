The Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) released the
Inpatient Prospective Payment System (IPPS) final rule August 2. The
rule establishes fiscal year (FY) 2018 policies for Medicare payments
to hospitals for inpatient stays occurring between October 1, 2017
